Tim's Hearty Vegetable Soup Recipe


For this recipe you'll need a large soup pot, preferably something quite large! For the vegetables use what you like, it should taste great no matter what you choose to put into it.

Amount Ingredients
4 to 5 Onions
3 or more Cloves of Garlic
12 Tomatos
A few tbsp Mushroom Stock Powder
10 to 20 Button Mushrooms
2 to 3 Bell Peppers
5-6 Waxy Potatos
1/2 Cabbage
Any Amount Extra Veggies


Step Instructions
1

Onions and Garlic to the pot. Over low heat brown the onions. Chop your tomatos into quarters or even eighths while they cook.
2

Add all your tomatos, the Mushroom stock powder, and maybe half the pot full of water. Season to taste. Mash the tomatos down until they disintegrate into the liquid.
3

Chop up your remaining ingredients to bite sized pieces and add them to the pot! I usually end with my potatos and cabbage and keep checking the seasoning (add more salt and pepper if too bland!).
4

Check that all the ingredients are cooked through, especially the potatos and cabbage. If they arent, just give them time over medium low heat until they are!
5 Enjoy!
